Marc Shave

I'm a creative developer and builder who works across app development, video production, AI-powered creative projects, and digital marketing. I take ideas from concept to completion — whether that's a full-stack mobile app, a short-form video, or a generative AI workflow.

A core part of how I work is leveraging AI through prompting — using it to research, plan, draft, generate and produce across every discipline I work in. This isn't a shortcut; it's a skill in itself, and one I've deliberately developed through both hands-on practice and formal certification.

My background is in creative media — I studied at Strode College and achieved a D*D*D* in Creative Media Production, giving me a strong foundation in storytelling, production, and visual communication that informs everything I do today.

How I use AI

AI is a core tool in my creative and technical workflow — not a replacement for skill, but an extension of it.

🤖 AI-Driven Workflow
Across every discipline I work in, I use AI through prompting as a deliberate, structured part of my process. This includes researching topics deeply and quickly, planning project structure, drafting scripts and copy, generating visual assets, prototyping ideas, and refining outputs. On Seekive, this spans the entire product — from AI-generated app imagery to real-time recommendations powered by the Gemini API. On video projects, AI assists with research, scripting, shot planning, and production decisions.
