About Me

Marc Shave

I'm a creative designer and developer who works across app development, video production, AI-powered creative projects, and digital marketing. I take ideas from concept to completion — whether that's a full-stack mobile app, a short-form video, or a generative AI workflow.

A core part of how I work is leveraging AI through prompting — using it to research, plan, draft, generate and produce across every discipline I work in. This isn't a shortcut; it's a skill in itself, and one I've deliberately developed alongside formal certification in generative AI and image generation from Google Cloud.

My background is in creative media — I studied at Strode College and achieved a D*D*D* Triple Distinction in Creative Media Production and a D* Distinction in IT, giving me a strong foundation in storytelling, production, and technical thinking that informs everything I build today.

My flagship product is Seekive — an AI-powered discovery app built end-to-end in React Native with Firebase and Google Gemini, live on iOS and Android, and shortlisted for the SMEnews UK Enterprise Awards 2026.

How I use AI

AI is a deliberate, structured part of my workflow across every discipline — not a replacement for craft, but an extension of it.

🤖 AI-Driven Workflow
Across everything I work on, I use AI through prompting as a core part of my process. On Seekive this spans the entire product — from real-time AI recommendations via the Gemini API to AI-generated imagery used throughout the app. On video and creative projects, AI assists with research, scripting, shot planning, and asset generation. I have formal Google Cloud certifications in both Generative AI and Image Generation, and treat prompting as a distinct technical skill.
