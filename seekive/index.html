AI-Generated Imagery

Images created using AI generation workflows and prompting — produced to illustrate activities and experiences within the app

Images were produced using AI image generation tools, guided by custom prompts crafted to visually represent specific activities, moods, and environments within the Seekive app. AI was also used throughout the project to research, plan, draft content, and generate ideas across development and design.

Features & Capabilities

What Seekive does under the hood

⊕
SpinWheel Interface
Signature compass-style spin wheel — a custom-built interaction that makes activity discovery feel playful and intuitive.
🤖
Gemini AI Recommendations
Real-time activity suggestions powered by Google Gemini — personalised to mood, time, budget, weather, and location.
🎨
AI-Generated Imagery
Custom images created through AI generation workflows, illustrating activities and experiences throughout the app.
👥
Social Layer
Friends system, messaging, community pages and shared discovery — turning solo exploration into a social experience.
🏆
Achievements & Gamification
An achievement system that rewards users for discovering new experiences and completing moments.
📓
Journal & Planning
Users can log completed moments, save discoveries, and plan future activities in a personal journal.
🌤
Weather & Location Aware
Suggestions adapt in real time to the user's current location and live weather conditions.
💰
Freemium Monetisation
AdMob integration for free-tier users and a subscription model for premium access — a complete revenue architecture.
